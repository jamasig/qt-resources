Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our Quranic Readings App collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

What We DON'T Collect

  • No Personal Information: We don't collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any personal data
  • No User Accounts: No registration required
  • No Analytics: We don't track your usage
  • No Location Data: We don't access your location

What We Store Locally

The following is stored only on your device and never sent anywhere:

  • Your app settings and preferences
  • Quranic database files
  • Arabic font files
  • Update history and preferences
Important: All your data stays on your device and is never shared with anyone.

Internet Usage

Our app only connects to the internet for:

  • Checking for app updates on GitHub
  • Downloading updates when you choose to

We don't send any of your personal data during these connections.

Data Security

  • All data is stored securely on your device
  • Downloads are encrypted and verified
  • No data is transmitted to our servers

Your Rights

Since everything is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • You control all your settings
  • You choose when to update

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for all ages since we don't collect any personal information from anyone.

Third-Party Services

We only use GitHub to check for updates. No other third-party services are used.
